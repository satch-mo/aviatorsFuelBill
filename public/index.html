Aviator's Calculator







WARNING: this tool is to be used strictly for reference. When estimating fuel consumption, there are a variety of factors that are to be taken into account such as altitude, prop pitch, fuel/air mixture, wind direction, higher fuel burn during climb, engine condition, engine rpms..

ALWAYS make sure you have an adequate reserve to get you to an alternative destination should there be a reason your initial destination becomes unapproachable mid-flight.
