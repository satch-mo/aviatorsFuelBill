Aviator's Calculator







WARNING: this tool is to be used strictly for reference and is based on averages of plane data. When estimating fuel consumption there are a variety of factors that are to be taken into account, such as engine RPM, altitude, prop pitch, fuel/air mixture, wind direction, higher fuel burn during climb, engine condition, etc.

ALWAYS make sure you have an adequate reserve to get you to an alternative destination should there be a reason your initial destination becomes unapproachable mid-flight.

Remember, takeoffs are optional but landings are mandatory. Happy flying!
