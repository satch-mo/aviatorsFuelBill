Aviator's Calculator







WARNING: this tool is to be used strictly for reference. When estimating fuel consumption there are a variety of factors to be taken into account, such as engine RPM, altitude, prop pitch, fuel/air mixture, wind direction, higher fuel burn during climb, or even engine condition.

ALWAYS make sure you have an adequate reserve to get you to an alternative destination should there be a reason your initial destination becomes unapproachable mid-flight.

Remember, takeoffs are optional but landings are mandatory! Happy flying!
